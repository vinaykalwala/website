How to raise a Child in a good way for a next better generation

Important things Parenting is one of the most important things to look after in this updated generation. Especially, One should remember that raising a child is not only an art but also building the next generation. Perhaps, it adds more weight to every single parent’s heart to observe their kid very keenly.

Even though it is not an easy ladder to climb up as a parent to your little baby, it takes a bit more patience, and maturity to be a good even exemplary dad or mom.

Steps to become a good ideal parent:

1.    Time:

Giving some time to your child not only puts you and your kid in a good and healthy mental state but also plays a vital role in a young kid’s mindset too. Most of the Surveys give results that the relationship between parents and children provides a happy, and stress-free adulthood whereas ill-parenting can cause a diplomatic, low-self esteemed teenager. Spending some time with young kids from your scheduled life can totally change their life.

2.    Communication:

Children’s Sharing of thoughts and expressing feelings depends on communication with their parents only. Because if a mother or father does not care about their explanation then the kids lose interest to share it with others.

 As a parent, if you are having a ton of issues but also you should never let that affect the affection between you and your kid. Simple, if you do not communicate with him then he does not know whom to open up to it then all of a sudden insecurity starts to impact that kid.

3.    Importance:

It is very important for a father and mother to give equal importance to every kid. It is natural that children do not have equal IQ levels, some are good at some things while others even do not show interest. If you have multiple kids in the family, obviously, they will have different lifestyles. So as a parent you should not judge them based on their grades or by sports results, all you have to do is to motivate them and show them the best possible way to make them achieve whatever they want in their life.

4.    Awareness:

Fathers or Mothers should really not know each and every detail of what their children doing. But at least have an idea about it. Parents must have clarity about their kid's behavior, mentality, the capacity of their strength, and ability to do something. So that they can have a clear picture if their kids are being true to them or not.

5.    Strict but be in limit:

This is the key aspect of parenting because it takes a lot of skill and mind presence to be strict with your kid and be within limits. A lot of surveys suggest that due to the strict parents only there are a lot of kids are becoming liars.

6.    Priorities:

Every working parent should definitely be aware of the priorities. Sometimes they have to prioritize between choosing work over kids. This importance plays a significant role in growing a kid’s mindset. If you keep on choosing to go to work instead of being with the child at parent’s meetings, visiting the zoo, or having a coffee with cookies can create a harsh feeling in you, which they may not know how to tell you about it.

7.    Be Honest:

A lot of people have been saying for a long time that “kids do not listen to what you say, they do what you do.” Actually, it does true that as a parent your behavior is a blueprint for a kid to look for. So, be honest and become a good role model to your kids. A child’s way of expressing, or even way of living is a reflection of the parent’s lifestyle. So, give your kids a bit of hope to look for when they need you the most not only by your words but also by your actions too.

8.    Space:

You must give enough space for your child to flourish if you want to become an ideal parent. Do not put pressure on your kids by interrupting their decision-making. In the end, Just ensure they are making good and right decisions at every point of their life.

9.    DO Not Compare:

Never ever compare your kid with another one. Because every kid is unique. Let them for who they are. Just accepting the kid as himself rather than forcing him to fill another’s shoes will definitely, not only kills the skillset of that person but also make you an evil parent. Remembered, that once you were compared so you did not recognize your own potential for a long time till you find out for yourself. Do not let this comparison affect your kid's mental ability, and strength.

Love:

One and only important thing that as a father or mother you could give your kid is your unlimited love. Just let them know whatever you do right now is for themselves only. If you are doing an overnight job or an extra shift to provide a school fee for your 6-year-old kid, just tell them that you are doing it for them, and just explain to them it is for their better future. It is very difficult to understand an adult about a family situation but as a kid, if he/she understands the sacrifices you make for themselves will definitely, be remembered in their small brains and will give you the best possible results in the future.

Overall:

Parenting is difficult, even feeding a kid without the internet has become a big pain for new, modern parents but you have to remember that you are raising a whole new generation.

If you want to see fewer old-age homes, a lot of respect for the elderly, stress-free and happy life, teach your child about moral values, and ethics, or you will be facing a lot of difficulties in the future with them. If you are thinking about joining your kid in a hostel to continue his higher studies without making him the necessary, then be ready to go to an old-age home in the future, because that’s what happens without proper guidance.

 Treat your kid 100 percent heartfully, then be ready to get treated likewise. Or else it’s their turn in near future to make their own decisions.

“Make sure that every decision you may take in your kid's life will be reciprocated in the future in one or another way.” So, why make a bad choice, if you have a lot of good things to take away.

“Every parent should let their child know that they have been working hard, putting in a lot of effort to give them the best possible they could.” If this could happen will see a better future tomorrow.
